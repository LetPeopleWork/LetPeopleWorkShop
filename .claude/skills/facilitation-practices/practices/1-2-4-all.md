# 1-2-4-All

- **Purpose:** Engage every voice at once in generating ideas; escalate from private reflection to shared sense-making so no single person dominates.
- **When to use:** Surfacing problems, ideas, or questions where you need broad, honest input and want to neutralize hierarchy / loud voices.
- **Group config:** Self (1) → pairs (2) → quads (4) → whole group (All).
- **Timing:** ~25 min (1 min self, 2 min pairs, 4 min quads, 5-10 min harvest + transitions). Trim to ~18 min.
- **Medium fit:**
  - In-person: silent self-note on stickies → pairs → join two pairs → wall harvest. Excellent.
  - Miro: private frames → breakout pairs → merge frames → cluster on board.
  - Video: self in chat-draft → 2-person breakout → 4-person breakout → plenary share.
- **Steps:**
  1. Pose ONE clear question. 1 min silent individual reflection (write it down).
  2. 2 min in pairs — build on each other's ideas.
  3. 4 min in quads — notice what stands out, what's shared/different.
  4. Whole group: each quad shares one idea that stood out. Harvest visibly.
- **Facilitator notes:** The structural magic against power dynamics — everyone has already committed a view privately before any senior person speaks, so dissent survives. Insist on the silent-write step; it's what protects the quiet voices.
